PortMaster progressor notice
============================

`tools/progressor` is bundled only to show progress during first-run extraction.
It comes from the community PortMaster extraction flow, reused from the
"TMNT: Shredder's Revenge" PortMaster method.

Credits to the PortMaster project and the original port authors.

`gptokeyb` is not bundled in this package. If present, the launcher uses the
copy supplied by the user's PortMaster/CFW installation.
